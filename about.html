We're Keplerians

We are a passionate team that participated in the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2024, driven by a shared love for space exploration and cutting-edge technology. Our team's mission was to create an innovative and interactive web app an orrery that simulates the real-time movement of celestial bodies including planets, Near-Earth Asteroids (NEAs), comets, and Potentially Hazardous Asteroids (PHAs). By leveraging NASA's small-body database, we aimed to provide an immersive experience that brings the wonders of our solar system to anyone with an internet connection.
